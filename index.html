من نحن · About the Group

Forty-five years of building

Al-Baz Group is a family-founded Iraqi group headquartered in Baghdad. Since 1980 we have built companies across food, tobacco, trading, real-estate investment, and hospitality — employing hundreds of Iraqis.

Milestones
1980

Al-Baz founded in Baghdad; the first production line starts and the Al-Baz brand is born.

1990s

Food production expands with the establishment of Baz Ready-Made Foods Ltd.

2000s

Entry into the molasses-tobacco sector through Al-Baz Molasses Tobacco Production.

2010s

Expansion into general trading and real-estate investment through Modern Visions and Tayeb Al-Khair.

Today

Five companies, 600+ employees, and the newest addition — Beethoven Restaurant & Café.

What we stand for

Quality first

Every product and service carries the family name. Nothing leaves us that we wouldn't offer at our own table.

Iraqi enterprise

We believe in building in Iraq, with Iraqi hands. Local employment and local suppliers are at the core of every decision.

Continuity

Through decades of change, Al-Baz never stopped. Those who build with us build on ground that holds.

Family stewardship

Now in its second generation, the group is managed with a long view — investing in people and ventures for the next forty years.

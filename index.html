من نحن · About the Group

Forty-five years of making things

Al-Baz Group is a family-founded Iraqi industrial group headquartered in Baghdad. Since 1980 we have built food and packaging businesses that employ hundreds of Iraqis and supply markets nationwide.

Milestones
1980

Al-Baz founded in Baghdad; the first chips production line starts running and the Al-Baz brand is born.

1992

Al-Baz Molasses opens, pressing traditional dibis from Iraqi dates for local and regional markets.

2005

Rebuilding and modernization — new imported production lines and expanded facilities in the Baghdad industrial zone.

2014

Al-Baz Packaging launches, bringing film and container production in-house and serving outside clients.

2019

Al-Baz Trading & Distribution formed to run the group's fleet, warehouses, and wholesale network.

Today

Four companies, 600+ employees, and products on shelves in all 18 governorates and neighboring markets.

What we stand for

Quality first

Every product carries the family name. Raw materials are sourced carefully, lines are inspected daily, and nothing leaves the plant that we wouldn't serve at our own table.

Iraqi industry

We believe in making things in Iraq, with Iraqi hands. Local employment, local suppliers, and local pride are at the core of every expansion decision.

Continuity

Through decades of change, Al-Baz never stopped producing. Our partners know that when they build with us, they build on ground that holds.

Family stewardship

Now in its second generation of leadership, the group is managed with a long view — investing in people and plants for the next forty years, not the next quarter.

Al-Baz Chips

شيبس الباز

The company that started it all. Al-Baz Chips produces potato chips and extruded snacks in a range of flavors developed for the Iraqi palate, from classic salt to chili-lime. Modern frying, seasoning, and nitrogen-flush packing lines keep every bag factory-fresh from Baghdad to the border.

  • Potato chips, corn snacks, and extruded puffs in 12+ flavors
  • Automated frying and seasoning lines with daily QC lab checks
  • Retail, family, and food-service pack sizes
  • Distribution to all 18 governorates through the group network

Al-Baz Molasses

دبس الباز

Dibis — Iraqi date molasses — is one of the country's oldest foods, and Al-Baz Molasses makes it the traditional way at industrial scale. Premium dates from Iraqi palm groves are pressed, filtered, and bottled with no additives, preserving the deep amber color and authentic flavor of true dibis.

  • 100% pure date molasses with no added sugar or preservatives
  • Glass jars, PET bottles, and bulk drums for industrial buyers
  • Sourcing partnerships with date farms across southern Iraq
  • Export-ready certification and private-label production

Al-Baz Packaging

الباز للتعبئة والتغليف

Built to guarantee the group's supply chain, Al-Baz Packaging now serves manufacturers across Iraq. The plant produces printed flexible film, laminates, and food-grade containers — the same materials that keep Al-Baz chips crisp and molasses sealed.

  • Printed flexible film and multi-layer laminates up to 8 colors
  • Food-grade PET and PP containers, jars, and closures
  • In-house design studio for brand and label development
  • Contract packaging services for third-party brands
